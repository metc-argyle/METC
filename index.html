What We Do

METC provides strategic consulting services to support businesses and organizations in advancing clean energy and sustainability goals. We help clients navigate the energy transition through modeling and practical solutions in decarbonization, clean energy strategy, and operational resilience.

We are based in Texas, USA.

About us

1. Clean Energy Transition Strategy
We help businesses, utilities, and organizations develop practical clean energy transition roadmaps aligned with sustainability targets, and long-term operational goals.
2. Decarbonization and Net-Zero Advisory
Our consulting services support clients in identifying carbon reduction opportunities, improving energy efficiency, and implementing achievable net-zero and emissions reduction strategies.
3. Renewable Energy Project Support
We provide guidance on renewable energy initiatives including solar, wind, battery storage, and emerging clean technologies, from feasibility assessment through implementation planning.
4. Energy Market & Policy Analysis
We deliver insights into evolving energy markets, industry trends, and regulatory developments to help clients make informed strategic and investment decisions.
5. Sustainability & Operational Resilience
We assist organizations in strengthening operational resilience by integrating sustainable practices, risk management approaches, and future-ready energy solutions into their business operations.
